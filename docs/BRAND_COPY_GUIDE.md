## SECTION 1 — ACTIVE FOR AIM STUDIO LITE V1

Build and use this copy now.

---

### Brand Voice & Tone

**Voice traits:**
- Bold, not arrogant
- Visionary, but grounded
- Inclusive — everyone has a way in
- Cinematic — words feel like film, not tech
- Direct — short sentences hit harder
- Confident — we know what we're building

**Avoid:**
- Buzzwords: "revolutionize," "disrupt," "synergize," "leverage," "best-in-class," "solutions"
- Generic AI hype: "powered by AI," "AI-driven"
- Hedging: "we hope," "we try to," "we aim to"

**Use instead:**
- "We make" not "We aim to make"
- "The future of cinema" not "Innovative AI solutions"
- "Be part of it" not "Get involved"

**Key copy rules:**
1. Never use buzzwords (revolutionize, disrupt, leverage, synergize, etc.)
2. Always use "We make" not "We aim to make"
3. Headlines should hit. Sub-headlines should explain.
4. CTAs should be specific action verbs — never "Learn More" alone
5. The signature line "DON'T LOOK AWAY." appears in footer only
6. Tagline "Cinema, reimagined." appears in footer and meta tags

## SECTION 2 — FUTURE / DO NOT BUILD YET

These pages and features are defined here for reference only. Do not build any of this in v1.

---

### Pages — Do Not Build

- **Upcoming / What's Next** — in-production and releasing-soon film announcements
- **Casting Page** — open casting calls, talent profile submission
- **Training / Learn the Craft** — free courses on AI filmmaking, screenwriting, directing
- **Submit a Script** — screenplay submission with AI analysis
- **Sponsors Page** — sponsor tiers, brand partnership
- **Donate Page** — one-time support, suggested amounts ($5 / $25 / $100 / Custom)

### Systems — Do Not Build

- Full course / badge / learning system
- Casting application and matching system
- Script submission and AI analysis pipeline
- Email newsletter / subscription system
- Sponsor tier management
- Community / comments system
- Watch party system
- Payment / subscription / donation flow

### Notification Emails — Do Not Write Yet

Do not build transactional emails until the email system is configured:
- Welcome email
- New film release notification
- Casting call opened notification
- Course completed notification
- Comment reply notification

### Copy — Reserved for Future Pages

The following copy exists in the full brand guide but must NOT be placed on the platform until the page is ready to ship:

- Upcoming Page: "What's Next / The films we're making right now."
- Casting Page: "Real roles. Real productions. We cast continuously."
- Training Page: "The future of filmmaking is in your hands. We're teaching it for free."
- Submit a Script: "Tell Us Your Story / Submit your screenplay."
- Sponsors Page: "The People Backing the Future"
- Donate Page: "Fuel the Films / Independent cinema runs on belief."
- Three Ways In section (Watch / Be In It / Learn to Make It) — only build when Casting and Training are live
